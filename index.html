About Me

From First-Time Founder to Serial Builder

I started my first company at 22 with $5,000 and a laptop. Since then, I've built and sold three companies across SaaS, marketplace, and fintech verticals — learning hard lessons so you don't have to.

Today I invest, advise, and build alongside exceptional founders. My edge is the intersection of product intuition, growth mechanics, and capital markets. I've raised from Sequoia, Andreessen, and Benchmark — and I can help you navigate those conversations.
